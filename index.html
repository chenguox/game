About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival game where you must defeat endless waves of monsters. As time progresses, the monsters become stronger and more numerous, putting your survival skills to the ultimate test.

Choose from different characters, each with unique abilities and strengths. Collect experience points to level up and enhance your skills. Pick up various power-ups throughout the game to gain an advantage against the monster hordes.

The longer you survive, the higher your score. Can you set a new record and become the ultimate Monster Survivor?

How to Play

Controls

  • Move: WASD or Arrow Keys
  • Attack: Automatic
  • Collect Items: Move over them
  • Level Up: Select upgrades from the menu

Objectives

  • Survive as long as possible against waves of monsters
  • Collect experience orbs to level up
  • Choose upgrades wisely to enhance your character
  • Defeat boss monsters for special rewards

Tips and Strategies

Early Game

  • Focus on movement speed upgrades first
  • Stay in open areas to avoid being cornered
  • Prioritize collecting experience orbs

Mid Game

  • Balance between offensive and defensive upgrades
  • Start focusing on area-of-effect attacks
  • Keep an eye out for health pickups

Late Game

  • Maximize damage output with synergistic upgrades
  • Use terrain features to funnel monsters
  • Save healing items for emergency situations
